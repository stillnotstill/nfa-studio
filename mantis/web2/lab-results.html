What is a COA?

A Certificate of Analysis is the lab report for one specific batch. An independent lab measures exactly what is inside: total CBD, total THC, and a screen for the things you never want, like pesticides, heavy metals, and residual solvents.

If it is on our shelf, it has a certificate. No exceptions.

b

Broad spectrum, plainly.

Broad spectrum means the extract keeps a wide range of the hemp plant's naturally occurring compounds while the THC is removed.

Non-detect means the lab's instruments found no measurable THC in the batch. Our seltzer is broad spectrum and tests non-detect, every batch.
