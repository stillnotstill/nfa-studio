Our Story

It started withvegetables.

Katrina has been growing plants for as long as she can remember. After college she traveled the world working on organic vegetable farms, and it was on a cannabis farm in southern Oregon that everything clicked. Her farming experience carried her quickly from field hand to head grower of a large-scale farm, learning alongside her crop each season.

I.
Plate I · from the flower press
Chapter I · 2018

Maine

In search of a new home, she landed in Maine in 2018, planning to leave cannabis growing behind. Then someone offered her a greenhouse. In 2020, Mantis Medicine became the first female-owned medical cannabis farm to earn MOFGA certification.

II.
Plate II · the winter bouquet
Chapter II · 2022

The turn to CBD

Around that time Katrina planted a couple of CBD plants for herself. Blending CBD with THC in topicals, she saw how much the two plants could do together, and her first salves became Christmas presents. The requests never stopped coming, and the most common one was for something without THC. Mantis Medicinals was born in 2022.

Every product in the wellness line exists because people kept asking for something they couldn't find anywhere else. Each season the garden tipped further toward CBD, until it was clear where the future was.

III.
Plate III · the certified season
Chapter III · 2025

The standard

Organic and local ingredients only. Plastic-free packaging wherever it's possible. Small batches, made by hand, third-party tested with every result published. In 2025, the farm earned its MOFGA organic certification, the same standard Katrina has farmed to since her vegetable days.
