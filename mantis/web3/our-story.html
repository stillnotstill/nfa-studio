Our Story

It all began with a seed.

Katrina has been growing plants for as long as she can remember. When she met cannabis in 2010, she knew this plant was different, and she's been growing in living soil ever since.

Mantis Medicinals started in 2019 with a batch of CBD salve made as Christmas gifts. Friends kept asking for more. Word spread the way it does in Maine, from kitchen to kitchen, and a farm brand was born.

Chapter i.

The Farm

Everything starts in Montville, Maine. Real earth, real roots, no shortcuts. We grow in living soil because that's how plants are meant to grow, and we're MOFGA Certified Organic, a standard we're proud to print on every label.

We were the first female-owned farm in our region to earn MOFGA's Clean Cannabis certification. That history carries into everything we make today.

Montville, Maine · Living Soil · MOFGA Certified Organic
II.
Plate II. · Gathered for the infusion
Chapter ii.

The Craft

Small batches, made by hand. Our salves and body oils begin with thirteen organic herbs solar-infused in olive oil for a full month, blended with local beeswax. Our seltzer is a sparkling infusion of organic botanicals and broad spectrum hemp extract, brewed like tea, finished like a craft beverage.

Katrina personally uses every product she makes. If it doesn't belong in her own routine, it doesn't leave the farm.

Small Batch · Solar-Infused · Hand Crafted

The Standard

Every batch is third-party lab tested, and every result is published. Broad spectrum means all of the plant's calm and none of the THC. We're also committed to plastic-free packaging, because clean should mean clean all the way through.
