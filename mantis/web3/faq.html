Part One

The Plant

i.What is broad spectrum CBD?+

The hemp plant makes more than just CBD. Broad spectrum keeps the plant's supporting botanicals, the terpenes and minor cannabinoids that naturally grow alongside it, while the THC is removed to non-detectable levels.

In plain terms: everything we love about the plant stays in the can, and the part people worry about doesn't.

ii.Will it get me high?+

No. Broad spectrum means the THC has been removed to non-detectable levels. You get the calm ritual, not a buzz.

Pour it over ice at lunch. You'll still be sharp for the two o'clock.

iii.How much CBD is in each can?+

20mg of broad spectrum CBD in every 12oz can.

Everyone's routine is their own, so start where it feels right for you. We make the seltzer. The ritual is yours.

iv.Is it lab tested?+

Every batch, by an independent third-party lab. The results are published on our Lab Results page, and the QR code on every can points straight there.

Scan it, read it, and know exactly what's in your glass.

Part Two

The Craft

i.Where is it made?+

Everything starts in living soil in Montville, Maine, MOFGA Certified Organic. The botanicals are brewed like a tea, then finished like a craft beverage: carbonated, canned, and sent out in small batches.

Farm first, beverage second. That order matters to us.

ii.What does it taste like?+

Like the garden, honestly. Real botanicals and barely any sugar.

Blueberry & Lavender is soft and berry-forward. Hibiscus & Lemon is bright and a little tart. Lime & Mint is cool and green, straight off the stem.

iii.Is the packaging recyclable?+

Yes. Our seltzer comes in aluminum cans, one of the most recyclable materials there is, and we're committed to keeping plastic out of our packaging.

Rinse it, bin it, feel fine about it.
