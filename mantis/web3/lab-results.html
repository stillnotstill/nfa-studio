Reading the Ledger

What the paperwork means.

i.

What a COA Is

A Certificate of Analysis is a report from an independent laboratory, not from us. It measures exactly how much CBD and which other cannabinoids are in a batch, and screens for what should not be there, including pesticides, heavy metals, and residual solvents.

Every batch gets its own certificate, tied to the batch number printed on the label.

ii.

Broad Spectrum & Non-Detect

Broad spectrum means the extract keeps a range of the hemp plant's naturally occurring compounds while the THC is removed. Non-detect means the lab's instruments found no measurable THC in the sample at all.

Our seltzer is made to that standard, and the certificate is how you check our work.
