What Is a COA?

A Certificate of Analysis is a report from an independent laboratory documenting exactly what is in a specific batch: how much CBD and other cannabinoids it contains, plus screening for things that should not be there, like heavy metals, pesticides, and residual solvents.

Every Mantis label carries a batch number. Match it to the table above and you can read the full report for the exact batch in your hand.

❦

Broad Spectrum &
Non-Detect

Broad spectrum means the extract keeps a range of the hemp plant's naturally occurring cannabinoids and botanical compounds while THC is removed to non-detectable levels.

Non-detect means the lab's instruments found no measurable THC in the sample, down to the smallest amount the test can register. Our full spectrum products keep the plant's trace THC, always below the federal limit of 0.3 percent.
