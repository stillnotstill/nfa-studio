About

About me

Joshua Peters is a creative director, producer, and content strategist based in Los Angeles. He builds content operations from scratch, scales them, and leads the teams that run them.

The work is rooted in one idea: content only matters if someone actually watches it. That means strategy before cameras roll, systems that keep content moving after the shoot wraps, and equal attention to distribution and production.

For the past three years, Joshua has served as Creative Director at Ankord Media, where he built and ran the entire content operation for founder Milan Kordestani. YouTube, podcast, shorts, documentary, book launches, influencer campaigns, paid ads. Multi-cam productions directed nationwide, post-production managed across editors on four continents. Every project, end to end.

Before that, over a decade in Web3 and crypto, producing content and building communities for GOGOcoin and Keom (formerly 0VIX), and leading marketing for Fatties NFT. Creative Director at Mantis Medicinals, owning the full brand from identity and packaging through social and PR.

Earlier in his career, Joshua worked in the White House Photography Office under the Obama Administration and shot professionally for Vail Resorts.

Currently open to creative leadership roles in content, brand, and marketing.
